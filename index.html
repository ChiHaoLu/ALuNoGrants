About

The purpose of this initiative is twofold.

Firstly, it aims to give back to society what it has received, as I have been the beneficiary of much "unpaid" help from many around me. Therefore, I hope to contribute to those in need when I am able.

Secondly, I hope that many others can take my place in accomplishing things I cannot, such as playing music or undertaking various meaningful projects, as my time is limited.
